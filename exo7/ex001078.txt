\exercice{1078, gourio, 2001/09/01}

\enonce
Calculer l'inverse de :
$$
\left(
\begin{array}{llll}
1 & ... & ... & 1 \\
0 & 1 & ... & ... \\
... & 0 & 1 & ... \\
0 & ... & 0 & 1
\end{array}
\right) ,\left(
\begin{array}{llll}
1 & 2 & ... & n \\
0 & 1 & 2 & ... \\
... & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
0 & ... & 0 & 1
\end{array}
\right) .
$$
\finenonce

\noindication

\nocorrection

\finexercice
