  \question{Soit $y \in \left]-\frac \pi2, \frac \pi2\right[$.
On pose $x = \ln\biggl(\tan\Bigl( \frac y2 + \frac \pi4\Bigr) \biggr)$.

Montrer que $\tanh \frac x2 = \tan \frac y2$,\quad
    $\tanh x = \sin y$,\quad $\ch x = \frac 1{\cos y}$.}
