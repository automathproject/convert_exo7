\enonce[$\sum\cos(kx)/\cos x^k = 0$]
R{\'e}soudre : $\sum_{k=0}^{n-1}\,\frac {\cos(kx)}{\cos^kx} = 0$.
\finenonce

\noindication

\correction
$x \equiv 0 (\mathrm{mod}\, {\frac\pi n})$, $x \not\equiv \frac \pi2 (\mathrm{mod}\, \pi)$.
\fincorrection

\finexercice
